TOPIC: VERB (BASIC)

In the sentence “She can sing beautifully but cannot dance well”, how are the two verbs “can sing” and “can(not) dance” used?
(A) modal, main
(B) main, modal
(C) main, main
(D) modal, modal
Answer: (D) modal, modal
Explanation:

“Can” and “cannot” are modal auxiliaries showing ability.​

“Sing” and “dance” are main verbs, but both verb phrases are headed by modals, so the function type asked is “modal, modal”.​

Explanation:

“Should” gives advice/obligation → modal auxiliary.​
